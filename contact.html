Our Goals

At Kitty Coleman Woodland Gardens, our mission is to cultivate a serene and inspiring natural sanctuary that showcases the beauty of rhododendrons and native flora. We are dedicated to providing a tranquil retreat for visitors, fostering a deep appreciation for nature's artistry, and supporting the arts through annual events that celebrate creativity and community engagement.
